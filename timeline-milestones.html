2. Detailed Milestones by Quarter

Q1 2026: Seed Close and Foundation

Product: Lock must have v1 scope in Jira. Spin up nightly UE5 build pipeline. Create Quest Auto Expander tech proof of concept with one test level. Build Alpha 0 plugin (context to dialogue). Start synthetic data generation run #1.

Commercial: Close seed round. Compile 40 studio long list (EU gameplay first studios). Warm intro emails through Dutch Game Garden, Walga, and Capital Games. Host 30 minute live demo for top 10 prospects via Zoom. Offer founding partner pilots at 20K each. Target: 6 NDAs signed, 2 on site demos booked.

Team: 4 FTE active (CEO, CTO, Chief Engineer at 0.3 FTE, Sales Director). Quarterly payroll: €93.6K.

Marketing: Stand up Discord with FAQ bot. Teaser tweet thread. Draft "Narrative Is Your Bottleneck" article. Publish article on Game Developer (gamedeveloper.com) and LinkedIn. Produce 60 second TikTok demo. Marketing spend: €10.3K.

Success Criteria:

  1. Seed round closed with minimum viable terms
  2. 40+ prospects logged in HubSpot with 6 NDAs signed
  3. Alpha 0 plugin functional in UE5 test environment

Q2 2026: Adoption, First Revenue

Product: Integrate quest branch visualiser in UE overlay. Finish dataset schema and vector DB build. Alpha 1: memory store and retrieval working. Synthetic data run #2 plus fine tune v0.2. Close 2+ pilot LOIs at 20K each.

Commercial: First revenue month (April 2026). Kick off pilot SOW and deliverables. Close 2+ pilot LOIs (20K each). Onsite workshop at partner #1 (Ghent or Utrecht). Install plugin in their dev branch. New customers: 17 (15 Solo, 2 Indie). Quarterly revenue target: €73.5K.

Team: 5 FTE (add Marketing/Sales at 0.1 FTE, Engineering at 0.3 FTE). Quarterly payroll: €160.4K.

Marketing: Release Loreweaver Lite to UE Marketplace (5K node cap). Lite plugin tutorial on YouTube (subtitled EN/FR/DE). Newsletter #1 ("3 Ways to Multiply Writers"). Record pilot studio testimonial teaser. Submit Nordic Game booth application. Marketing spend: €13.3K.

Success Criteria:

  1. 40K pilot cash invoiced
  2. Lite plugin live on UE Marketplace with 1,500+ installs and 25% weekly active users
  3. Pilot partner #1 generating quests in engine

Q3 2026: Adoption, Beta Delivery

Product: Implement localisation export flow (XML/CSV). Stability and crash telemetry. Alpha 2: branching quest generator with NAM controls. Open beta flag for Lite testers. Partner #1 mid pilot ROI review (target 25K+ words auto generated). Feature freeze for Beta 0. Begin formal QA cases.

Commercial: Partner #2 sprint review (remote). Collect writer hour saved metrics. Sign Pilot #3 (DACH). Initiate data export to train model on their lore. Start first AAA discovery call (a major European publisher). Enterprise warm intros: Asobo, Amplitude, Arrowhead. Send enterprise tech eval briefs. Quarterly revenue target: €72.7K.

Team: 6 FTE (add R&D at 0.3 FTE). Quarterly payroll: €194.7K.

Marketing: Grow Discord to 1,000 members. Launch French #canal-fr. Start monthly "Narrative Audit" Twitch stream. Twitter threads in DE and FR quoting partner feedback. Draft EU AI Act compliance white paper for AAA buyers. Secure Nordic Game booth (May). Marketing spend: €24.1K. Conferences: Nordic Game (demo station, 2.7K).

Success Criteria:

  1. 3 pilots active with 60K total collected
  2. Lite plugin at 3,000 installs
  3. Beta 0 crash rate below 2%

Q4 2026: Distribution, Conference Season

Product: Ship Beta 0 to pilots. Start localisation QA with partner data. Performance tuning pass (on device 4090 and 3070). Release Beta 1 to Lite beta channel. Reach RC 0 candidate status. Only P1 bug fixes after this point. Final fine tune v0.9 on blended dataset. Partners run full regression on RC 0.

Commercial: Gather NPS and case study quotes from partner #1. Offer paid Indie Jump Start bundle (2K) to wait list. Pre book 10 exec meetings for Gamescom (DE/FR/Nordic). At Gamescom/Devcom: live "Narrative Speedrun" booth, collect 400 badge scans, Devcom talk "Procedural Story at AAA Frame Budgets." 150 qualified SQLs in HubSpot. New customers: 32 (30 Solo, 1 Indie, 1 AA). Quarterly revenue target: €173.3K.

Team: 6 FTE. Quarterly payroll: €194.7K.

Marketing: Publish case study #1 as PDF and video. Devlog #3 on UE blog (guest post). Add "Lore Guardian" swag store. Gameforce Belgium booth (October). IndieCade Europe (Lyon). Run pre launch webinar (ENG/FR/DE subtitles). Marketing spend: €23.0K. Conferences: Gamescom mini booth (5K), Gameforce Belgium indie pod (0.8K), IndieCade Europe (0.75K).

Success Criteria:

  1. 50 Indie bundle wait list sign ups
  2. Publish case study with measurable download/view count
