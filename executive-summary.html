LoreWeaver B.V. | Netherlands | Pre-Seed Round | March 2026

The Opportunity

Players demand infinite content and meaningful choice. Studios still build narrative by hand, a process that does not scale and consumes 15 to 30% of AAA budgets (writing, voice production, localization, cinematics, and narrative QA combined; see Narrative Cost Model for the bottom-up derivation). AI inference costs have dropped 90% in two years, and small language models now run on consumer hardware. No incumbent has shipped an integrated authoring + runtime solution. The window is open.

The Product

Architect converts raw lore, world bibles, and design docs into game-ready narrative data: quests, dialogue trees, branching storylines. It delivers 100x faster lore-to-game-data conversion while keeping writers in full creative control. Architect is live in beta and powers real production workflows today.

Director generates live NPC behavior, dynamic plot, and adaptive dialogue at runtime, entirely on-device. Sub-3-second response times, zero cloud costs, zero data-privacy risk. Director runs locally on consumer hardware using optimized small language models.

Together they form the only integrated pipeline from narrative creation to in-game execution. Architect feeds Director. No competitor covers both.

Business Model

Architect is free to use. Revenue comes from AI token consumption at a 6x markup over base inference cost, the same model that drives AWS and Twilio. Studios pay only for what they use, eliminating procurement friction. Margins scale with adoption.

Director is free during development. Once a game ships and crosses €100K in revenue, LoreWeaver collects a 1% royalty on revenue above that threshold. This is far more studio-friendly than Unreal Engine's 5% rate, while following the same "pay on success" structure. LoreWeaver's success is aligned directly with the studio's commercial outcome.

Market Position

  • Only integrated pipeline. No competitor covers both authoring and runtime. LoreWeaver owns the full workflow.
  • On-device, not cloud. Inworld ($125M raised) requires cloud inference and has pivoted away from gaming. LoreWeaver runs locally with zero ongoing server costs for studios.
  • Incumbent lacks deep AI integration. articy:draft X added basic third-party AI extensions (dialogue generation, barks, preview images, voice synthesis), but these are bolt-on features that lack deep lore graph integration, structured data pipelines, or an AI-powered runtime. articy:draft ships basic runtime interpreters for Unity and Unreal, but offers no AI-powered runtime for dynamic narrative generation.
  • NVIDIA validates the thesis. ACE proves on-device AI is the direction of travel, but it is RTX-only infrastructure with no narrative tooling. LoreWeaver is the creative layer on top.
