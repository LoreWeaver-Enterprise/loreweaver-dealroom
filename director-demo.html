Director Demo v1.0

Real-time narrative runtime in action

Director takes structured content from Architect and brings it to life inside shipped games. It generates NPC dialogue, manages mood and trust systems, drives dynamic quests, and adapts to player choices. Everything runs on-device at 7.8 tokens/sec with complete privacy.
