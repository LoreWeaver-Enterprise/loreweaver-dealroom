University pilots

Two active pilots with Dutch higher-education programs. Both validate Architect as a teaching and prototyping tool, and give LoreWeaver structured access to graduating game-design and ICT talent.

InstitutionProgramWhat they are doing
Fontys ICTICT and Game Design, EindhovenStudent cohorts use Architect to structure narrative for capstone game projects. The pilot covers schema design, entity extraction, and engine export. Feedback is fed back into the product roadmap. Pilot also serves as a hiring funnel for the engineer hire post-round.
HvA (Hogeschool van Amsterdam)Game Development, AmsterdamNarrative design course using Architect to compare manual narrative structuring against AI-assisted workflows. Students publish their pipelines as portfolio pieces, which generates organic referrals to studios where they intern or get hired.
