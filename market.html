None of these companies own the narrative layer. The category is hot, and the LoreWeaver wedge sits in the gap nobody is funding.

AI compute deflation

Per-token inference cost is falling roughly 10x per year on consumer hardware. The economics of on-device narrative AI were unworkable in 2023. They are workable today. They will be trivial by 2027. Director was timed for this curve.

EU AI Act drives on-device demand

The EU AI Act, in force from August 2025, classifies game AI systems handling player data under transparency and data-minimization obligations. On-device inference (no player data leaves the machine) is the cleanest compliance path. Cloud NPC vendors face structural friction in EU markets. Director is on-device by design.
