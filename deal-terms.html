5. Legal Structure

LoreWeaver operates through a Dutch BV (Besloten Vennootschap) structure with three entities.

Entity Function
Holding BV Owns all intellectual property (the LoreWeaver platform, Architect, Director). IP sits here to protect it from operational risk. If the operating entity faces claims, the IP remains shielded.
Operations BV Signs all commercial contracts, employs staff, and runs day-to-day operations. Revenue flows through this entity. The Ops BV licenses IP from the Holding BV.
STAK (Stichting Administratiekantoor) A Dutch foundation that holds shares on behalf of certificate holders. The STAK serves three purposes: (1) it locks the company's mission, preventing short-term value extraction that conflicts with the long-term product vision; (2) it manages the employee option pool, issuing certificates (depositary receipts) rather than direct shares, which simplifies option grants and avoids complex share transfers; (3) it provides voting control to the board of the STAK, keeping governance clean while still giving economic rights to certificate holders.

This structure is standard for Dutch tech startups, particularly those managing an option pool and seeking to preserve founder control through early funding rounds.
